Strong programming background in Python, and JavaScript, with deep experience in PyTorch, Transformers

mRNA modeling, drug target interaction prediction and algorithmic fairness.

Passionate about building transparent, biologically grounded AI systems and expanding the capabilities of machine learning to drive discovery in the life sciences.
