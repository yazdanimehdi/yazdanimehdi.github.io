Jane Smith is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Technology. She leads the Smith Research Lab, focusing on developing novel machine learning methods for understanding and generating natural language.

## Research Interests

Her research spans several areas at the intersection of machine learning and human-computer interaction:

- **Natural Language Understanding**: Developing models that can comprehend nuanced language, including sarcasm, metaphor, and cultural context.
- **Interactive AI Systems**: Building AI tools that effectively collaborate with humans in creative and analytical tasks.
- **Fairness in ML**: Ensuring that machine learning models are equitable and transparent.
