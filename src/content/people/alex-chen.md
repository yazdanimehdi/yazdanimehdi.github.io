Alex Chen is a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Smith Research Lab. His research focuses on multimodal learning, combining vision and language understanding to build more capable AI systems.

## Research Focus

Alex's current work investigates how visual and textual information can be jointly leveraged to improve:

- Image captioning and visual question answering
- Cross-modal retrieval systems
- Multimodal generative models for creative applications
