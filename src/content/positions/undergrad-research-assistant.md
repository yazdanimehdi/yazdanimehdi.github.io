## Overview

We periodically offer research assistant positions for undergraduate students interested in gaining hands-on research experience in machine learning and natural language processing. These positions provide mentorship from senior lab members and the opportunity to contribute to ongoing research projects.

## Responsibilities

- Assist with data collection, annotation, and preprocessing
- Implement and run experiments under the guidance of graduate students or postdocs
- Participate in weekly lab meetings and paper reading groups
- Help maintain research codebases and documentation

## Requirements

- Currently enrolled undergraduate student in Computer Science or a related field
- Completed at least one course in machine learning, AI, or data science
- Proficiency in Python programming
- Available for at least 10 hours per week during the academic year (or full-time during summer)
- Self-motivated and eager to learn

## What We Offer

- Mentorship from experienced researchers
- Exposure to cutting-edge research topics
- Potential for co-authorship on publications
- Letters of recommendation for graduate school applications
- Flexible scheduling around classes

This position is currently closed. Please check back later or sign up for our mailing list to be notified when new positions become available.
