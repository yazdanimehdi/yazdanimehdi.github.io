## Overview

We are seeking a motivated and talented PhD student to join our research group working on cutting-edge Natural Language Processing. This fully funded position offers the opportunity to conduct research at the intersection of large language models, multilingual understanding, and controllable text generation.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct original research in NLP and publish at top-tier venues (ACL, EMNLP, NeurIPS, ICML)
- Develop novel methods for improving language model capabilities
- Collaborate with other lab members and external research partners
- Present research findings at international conferences and workshops
- Contribute to open-source research software and datasets

## Requirements

- M.S. in Computer Science, Computational Linguistics, or a related field (exceptional B.S. candidates will also be considered)
- Strong programming skills in Python and experience with PyTorch or JAX
- Familiarity with transformer architectures and modern NLP techniques
- Demonstrated research ability (publications preferred but not required)
- Excellent written and oral communication skills in English

## What We Offer

- Full tuition coverage and competitive stipend
- Access to state-of-the-art computing resources (GPU clusters)
- Travel funding for conferences and workshops
- A collaborative and supportive research environment
- Mentorship and career development opportunities
