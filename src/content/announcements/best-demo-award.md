Congratulations to Maria Garcia for receiving the **Best Demo Award** at NAACL 2024 for her interactive demonstration of a natural language-guided robot learning system!

The demo showcased a system where users can teach a robotic arm new tasks using only natural language instructions, with the robot providing real-time feedback about its understanding and confidence. The work demonstrates a practical approach to making robotics more accessible to non-expert users.
