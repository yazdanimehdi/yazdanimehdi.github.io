Welcome to the Smith Research Lab blog! We're excited to launch this platform to share updates about our research, thoughts on the field, and resources for the community.

## Who We Are

The Smith Research Lab is part of the Department of Computer Science at the University of Technology. Our team includes faculty, postdoctoral researchers, and graduate students working at the intersection of machine learning, natural language processing, and human-computer interaction.

## Our Mission

We believe in building AI systems that are:

1. **Useful** - Solving real problems for real people
2. **Understandable** - Transparent in their reasoning and limitations
3. **Equitable** - Fair and inclusive across diverse populations
4. **Open** - Sharing our tools, data, and findings with the community

## What to Expect

On this blog, you'll find:

- Research updates and paper summaries
- Tutorials and technical deep-dives
- Lab news and announcements
- Reflections on the field and its future

Stay tuned for more content, and feel free to [reach out](/contact) if you'd like to learn more about our work!
